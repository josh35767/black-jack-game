Black Jack

Instructions

  1. You are dealt a hand, each card worth their face values,
    face cards worth, 10, and aces worth 1 or 11.
  2. Your goal is to have a higher hand than the dealer,
    but if you get over 21, you automatically lose.
  3. Each round you bet 5 chips, winning will double your bet.
  4. You are given to choices, 'hit' or 'stand'
    Hitting will giving you a card, standing will end your turn.
  5. Once you reach 100 chips you win, reach 0 chips, and game over.

You win!
